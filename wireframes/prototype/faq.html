General

What age groups do you teach?
We teach ages 2 and up. Our Tiny Twisters classes are for ages 2-6, most weekday classes start at ages 7+, and our Open Cheerleading is for ages 16+.
Where are your classes held?
All classes are held at Grosvenor Street, Ashton-under-Lyne, OL7 0RG. There is free parking available nearby.
What should my child wear to class?
Comfortable clothing that allows free movement — leggings, shorts, t-shirt. No special shoes are needed for the first class. The coach can advise on footwear from the second session.
Can siblings or parents wait during class?
Yes, there is a waiting area. Parents of younger children (Tiny Twisters, ages 2-6) are welcome to stay nearby.
Do you offer SEN/SEND classes?
Yes. Our SEND Dance class on Wednesdays (5:30-6:30 PM, Ages 6+) is designed to be adaptive, inclusive and nurturing. All our coaches are trained to support additional needs across all classes.

Booking & Free Trials

How do I book a free trial?
Go to our Free Trial page, fill in your child's details and choose a class. No payment is needed. You can reschedule your trial once with 24 hours notice.
Do I need to create an account?
No. We do not use accounts. You book using your email address, and we verify bookings with a one-time code sent to that email. Simple and secure.
Can I try more than one class?
The free trial is for one class. After that, you can book into any class that suits your child's age and interests.
What happens after the free trial?
If your child enjoyed the class, you can book and choose a payment plan — drop-in, monthly or upfront (10% discount). No pressure.

Payments & Pricing

What payment methods do you accept?
We accept card payments (Visa, Mastercard), Apple Pay and Google Pay through Stripe.
What are the pricing options?
Weekday classes: Drop-in £6.50 per class, Monthly £30/month (10-month term), or Upfront £270 (10% discount).
Saturday classes: £5 per class, or £8 for both Tiny Twisters blocks (ages 2-6).
Prices shown are estimates and will be confirmed before launch.
Can I cancel my monthly plan?
Yes. Cancel before the next billing date to stop future charges. No refunds are issued for past attended sessions.
What is the refund policy for term payments?
If you paid upfront, you can request a refund within 7 days of purchase. The refund amount will be reduced by any classes already attended.

Classes & Schedule

What if my child misses a class?
Missed classes can be made up in another suitable class within 30 days, provided the age and level are appropriate. Contact us to arrange this.
Can my child switch to a different class?
Yes, contact us and we will move them to another class where space and suitability allows.
Are there performances or competitions?
We run showcases and events throughout the year. These are optional and details will be shared in class and via email.
What if we are running late?
Try to arrive on time so your child gets the most from the session. If you are occasionally late, just come in quietly — the coach will welcome them.

Still have a question?

We are happy to help. Get in touch and we will get back to you.
